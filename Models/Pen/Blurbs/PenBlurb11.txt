Next, the cam, which is no longer being pushed by the follower but is rather pushed the other way by the force of the contracted spring rams into the divet along the pen walls, which hold the cam in place at a distance such that the tip of the pen is at a comfortable distance out for writing. This step concludes one click that makes it go from in to out, off to on. Clicking once more initiates a similar process and retracts the tip again.
